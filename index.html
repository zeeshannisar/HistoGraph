HistoGraph brings together three computer science partners with three medical centres to develop novel techniques for automatic diagnosis from whole slide images. The project will develop a graph based framework that will use state-of-the-art stain invariant feature extraction techniques to integrate multi-modal information at the slide level to automatically predict grading severity.
